Getting Here

Parking

  • Street Parking: Metered parking available on Brewery Lane and surrounding streets
  • Public Garage: Downtown Parking Structure (2 blocks away) - $5 flat rate after 5pm
  • Bike Racks: Secure bike parking available in front of our building

Public Transit

  • MAX Light Rail: Pioneer Square Station (3 blocks)
  • Streetcar: NS Line stops at NW 10th & Glisan (4 blocks)
  • Rideshare: Uber and Lyft pickup/dropoff zone on Brewery Lane
