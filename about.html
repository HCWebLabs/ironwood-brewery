Our Story

A passion for craft beer and exceptional food since 2015

The Beginning

Where passion meets craft

Ironwood Brewing Co. was founded in 2015 by brewmaster Jake Morrison and chef Sarah Chen, two friends who shared a vision of creating a gathering place where exceptional craft beer and farm-to-table cuisine could come together.

Jake brought years of brewing experience from craft breweries across the Pacific Northwest, while Sarah honed her culinary skills at award-winning restaurants in Portland and Seattle. Together, they saw an opportunity to create something special‚Äîa brewery that would honor traditional brewing methods while pushing creative boundaries.

The name "Ironwood" comes from the dense, resilient trees that grow in the region. Like our namesake, we're rooted in our community and built to last. From our first batch brewed in a small warehouse space to our current location in the historic downtown district, we've remained committed to quality, innovation, and bringing people together.

Our Journey

A decade of brewing excellence and community

2015

Jake and Sarah open Ironwood Brewing Co. in a 2,000 sq ft warehouse with a 3-barrel brewing system. First batch: Copper IPA.

2016

Expanded to include a small kitchen and tasting room. Copper IPA wins Bronze at the Oregon Beer Awards.

2017

Moved to our current downtown location with a 15-barrel system and full restaurant. Launched our barrel-aging program.

2018

Midnight Stout wins Gold at the Great American Beer Festival. Featured in Craft Beer & Brewing Magazine.

2019

Expanded outdoor patio and added fire pits. Started our community giveback program, donating 1% of profits to local nonprofits.

2020

Launched curbside pickup and home delivery during pandemic. Created the "Support Local" beer series with neighboring restaurants.

2021

Installed solar panels covering 60% of our energy needs. Began canning operation for distribution to local bottle shops.

2022

Celebrated 100,000 barrels brewed. Launched monthly brewery tours and beer education classes.

2023

Named "Best Brewery in Portland" by Portland Monthly. Added private event space for community gatherings.

2024

Opened our hop garden in partnership with local farmers. Launched the Ironwood Fellowship program for aspiring brewers.

2025

Celebrating 10 years of craft beer excellence. Expanded distribution throughout the Pacific Northwest. Looking forward to the next decade!
