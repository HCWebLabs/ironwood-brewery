Event Spaces

Main Taproom

Capacity: 60-80 guests

Our main taproom features exposed brick, industrial lighting, and direct views of our brewing operations. Perfect for larger gatherings with a lively atmosphere.

  • Full bar access with 20 taps
  • Open layout for mingling
  • AV system for presentations
  • Brewery tours available

Private Dining Room

Capacity: 20-40 guests

An intimate space with rustic wood tables and warm lighting. Ideal for corporate dinners, rehearsal dinners, and smaller private celebrations.

  • Separate from main taproom
  • Dedicated server
  • Custom menu options
  • Private bar setup available
