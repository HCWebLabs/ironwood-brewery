Our Story

Brewing excellence since 2015

Founded by brewmaster Jake Morrison and chef Sarah Chen, Ironwood Brewing Co. was born from a shared passion for creating exceptional experiences through beer and food.

We brew small-batch, innovative beers using locally sourced ingredients and time-honored techniques. Our kitchen complements every pint with seasonally inspired dishes that showcase regional farmers and producers.

From our flagship Copper IPA to experimental barrel-aged stouts, every beer tells a story. Join us at the bar, gather around our communal tables, and become part of the Ironwood family.
